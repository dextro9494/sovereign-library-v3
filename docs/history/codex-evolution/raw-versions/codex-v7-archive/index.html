5.0 THE ARCHIVE: STRUCTURE & ACCESS

5.1 Archive Overview

The Sovereign Archive is the permanent, searchable memory of the sovereignty. It consists of five volumes:

  1. Volume 1: Canonical Narratives — All Stories, tagged, dated, cross-referenced.
  2. Volume 2: Council Intelligence — Themed, frozen dialogues with version context.
  3. Volume 3: Field Data Logs — Chronological test records, organized by quarter.
  4. Volume 4: Raw Chat Transcripts — Indexed by date and primary theme.
  5. Volume 5: Protocol Evolution Log — Version history, patch notes, rationales.

5.2 Migration Protocol

Triggers:

  1. Monthly (Last Sunday): Any Canonical Story older than one major version migrates to Archive Volume 1.
  2. Quarterly: Field Data Logs roll over to Archive Volume 3.
  3. On-Demand: Council Intelligence entries exceeding 3 per theme rotate to Archive Volume 2.

Cross-Reference Syntax:

· In Core: [See Archive: Vol.1, Story 004 (v6.1.1)] · In Archive: [Originated in Core v6.1.1, Section 5.1]

5.3 Archive Access Protocol

  1. Search First: Use indexed themes and tags.
  2. Trace Lineage: Follow cross-references to understand evolution.
  3. Extract, Don’t Drown: Retrieve needed intelligence without full immersion.
