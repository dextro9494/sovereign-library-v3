Summary: Three pillars used as decision criteria: Financial/Professional Sovereignty and Relational Sovereignty (with enjoyment as an outcome metric).

Source: Raw Transcripts: Batches 1–4

The Three Pillars#

Financial Sovereignty#

Enough for enjoyment, freedom, culture.

Professional Sovereignty#

Freelance/remote work, international mobility, autonomy.

Relational Sovereignty#

Authentic connection. A parallel build track, equal in priority to financial and professional goals.

Function#

These pillars serve as the decision filter for all actions (RCP).
